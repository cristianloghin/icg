ICG Units

ICG shares held in certificated form are represented by share certificates dated 10 June 2014 or later. Share certificates dated prior to 10 June 2014 were cancelled by the company and are no longer valid.

On 28 July 2003 the Company cancelled all then existing certificates covering holdings of ICG Ordinary Shares. These were replaced by certificates covering holdings of ICG Units. On 10 June 2014 new share certificates were issued following a sub-division of the ordinary share comprised in each ICG unit. After the 10 June 2014, only the new certificates are valid for sale / transfer purposes.

An ICG unit consists of one Ordinary Share and ten Redeemable Shares (or such lesser number thereof, if any, resulting from the redemption of one or more Redeemable Share).

If you have any query regarding your holding of ICG Units, please contact the Company Registrar, Computershare Investor Services (Ireland) Limited, P.O. Box 954, Dublin 18 or at www.computershare.com
