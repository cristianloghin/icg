Investment Case

Irish Continental Group is the leading Irish-based maritime transport group. We carry passengers and cars, Roll on Roll off freight and Container Lift on Lift off freight, on routes between Ireland, the United Kingdom and Continental Europe. We also operate container terminals in the ports of Dublin and Belfast.

2019 will be an exciting year as we promote our direct Dublin/ France service with our new vessel W.B. Yeats and continue to seek out improvement and investment opportunities for our longer term success.

Eamonn Rothwell, Chief Executive Officer 2018 Annual Report
